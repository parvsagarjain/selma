About the Founder

MEET
DAKSH RAI.

Founder & Inventor — SELMA, The Blaze Cap

Daksh Rai is the Founder and Inventor of SELMA — The Blaze Cap, a safety-focused mobility startup dedicated to making two-wheeler riding safer through innovation.

The spark

A rider you can’t see
is a rider at risk.

Driven by a passion for automobiles and engineering from a young age, Daksh identified a simple yet critical problem: motorcyclists are often difficult to see from behind, increasing the risk of accidents.

This inspired him to develop SELMA — a smart helmet with integrated brake lights and turn indicators that wirelessly synchronize with the motorcycle, enhancing rider visibility and road safety.

At just 17 years old, Daksh transformed the idea into a working prototype, independently handling its design, electronics, and development.

The vision

Make every ride
safer.

With a long-term vision of revolutionizing motorcycle safety, Daksh is working towards collaborating with leading motorcycle manufacturers to integrate smart safety technologies into future vehicles and riding gear. His goal is simple: to make every ride safer through practical, innovative engineering.
